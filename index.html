Open Domains

Free subdomains for personal sites, open-source projects, and more.

Open Domains is a service where you can register subdomains for free. Our service offers the opportunity to register subdomains such as foo.is-cool.dev or bar.is-not-a.dev and more! Our subdomains support the use of A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, NS and TXT records.
